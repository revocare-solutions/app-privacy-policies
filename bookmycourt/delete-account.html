Account Deletion

BookMyCourt — How to delete your account and data

How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your BookMyCourt account using any of the following methods:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the BookMyCourt app
  2. Go to Profile (bottom navigation bar)
  3. Tap Account Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to support@bookmycourt.com with:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address or phone number

We will process your deletion request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once complete.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Your favorite courts and saved preferences
  • Your search history and location preferences
  • Push notification tokens and device registrations

What May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for legal, regulatory, or business continuity purposes:

  • Booking Records: Historical booking transactions may be retained for up to 3 years for legal and accounting compliance.
  • Payment Records: Financial transaction records may be retained as required by applicable tax and financial regulations.
  • Reviews & Ratings: Reviews you submitted will be anonymized but may remain visible to help other users.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Any upcoming bookings at the time of deletion will be automatically cancelled.
  • Refund eligibility for cancelled bookings follows our standard cancellation policy.
  • Uninstalling the app does not delete your account — you must follow the steps above.

For questions about account deletion, contact us at support@bookmycourt.com.
