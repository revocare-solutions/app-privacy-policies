Privacy Policy

Glambook — Beauty & Salon Booking App  |  Last updated: March 31, 2026

Summary: Glambook collects only the data necessary to help you discover and book beauty and wellness services. We do not sell your data, do not serve ads, and do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number provided during registration.
  • Booking Data: Appointment history, service preferences, and cancellation records.
  • Location Data (Optional): Approximate location to show salons and spas near you.
  • Payment Data: Transaction records for bookings. Credit card details are processed by third-party payment providers.
  • Reviews & Ratings: Reviews and ratings you leave for salons and services.
  • Authentication Tokens: Securely stored tokens for maintaining your session.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you and maintain your secure session
  • Display available salons, spas, and beauty services
  • Process your appointments and send booking confirmations
  • Show salons and beauty professionals near your location
  • Send push notifications for appointment reminders and updates
  • Display your booking history and favorite salons
  • Process payments through our payment partners

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Authentication tokens stored using encrypted secure storage.
  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption).
  • Passwords never stored in plain text.
  • Payment information handled by PCI-DSS compliant processors.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

  • With Salon Owners: Your name and contact info shared when you make an appointment.
  • Service Infrastructure: Firebase for push notifications and crash reporting.
  • Payment Processors: Payment data shared with payment partners.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Permissions

  • Internet Access: Required for browsing and booking.
  • Push Notifications: Appointment reminders and updates.
  • Location (Optional): To show salons near you.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data retained while account is active.
  • Booking and payment records retained for minimum 3 years.
  • Account deletion processed within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Request account deletion (see Account Deletion)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Glambook is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
