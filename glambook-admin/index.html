Privacy Policy

Glambook Admin — Salon & Beauty Business Management App  |  Last updated: March 31, 2026

Summary: Glambook Admin collects only the data necessary to provide salon management and appointment booking features. We do not sell your data, do not serve ads, and do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number provided during registration.
  • Business Data: Salon/spa details, service menus, pricing, staff profiles, operating hours, and facility photos.
  • Appointment Data: Booking records, client appointments, schedules, cancellations, and booking history.
  • Financial Data: Revenue reports, settlement records, and payment transaction summaries.
  • Staff Data: Staff member profiles, roles, schedules, and permissions.
  • Location Data (Optional): Salon location for map display to clients.
  • Photos (Optional): Salon and service images you upload.
  • Authentication Tokens: Securely stored tokens for maintaining your session.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you and maintain your secure session
  • Enable appointment management, scheduling, and client management
  • Display your business dashboard, booking analytics, and revenue reports
  • Process settlement and payment information
  • Send push notifications for new appointments, cancellations, and updates
  • Allow clients to discover and book your services through the Glambook client app
  • Provide role-based access to features based on staff permissions

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Authentication tokens are stored using your device's encrypted secure storage.
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption).
  • We use role-based access control for staff members.
  • Your password is never stored in plain text.
  • Our servers are hosted on secure cloud infrastructure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

  • With Clients: Your salon name, location, services, pricing, and photos are visible to clients on the Glambook client app.
  • Service Infrastructure: Firebase (Google) for push notifications and crash reporting.
  • Payment Processors: Settlement data shared with payment partners.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Permissions

  • Internet Access: Required to communicate with servers.
  • Push Notifications: Real-time alerts for new appointments and updates.
  • Location (Optional): To set your salon location on the map.
  • Camera/Photo Library (Optional): To upload salon and service photos.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data retained while your account is active.
  • Booking and transaction records retained for minimum 3 years.
  • Account deletion processed within 30 days of request.

7. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Request account deletion (see Account Deletion)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Glambook Admin is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
