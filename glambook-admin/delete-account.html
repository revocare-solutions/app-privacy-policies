Account Deletion

Glambook Admin — How to delete your account and data

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the Glambook Admin app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Tap Account Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision

Option 2: Email Request

Email support@glambook.com with subject "Account Deletion Request" and your registered email.

Deletion processed within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Account profile and login credentials
  • Salon management settings and preferences
  • Staff associations and role assignments
  • Push notification tokens

What May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for legal/regulatory purposes:

  • Booking Records: Up to 3 years for compliance.
  • Settlement/Payment Records: As required by tax regulations.
  • Client Reviews: Anonymized but may remain visible.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Active appointments must be handled before deletion.
  • Uninstalling the app does not delete your account.
