Account Deletion

BookMyCourt Admin — How to delete your account and data

How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your BookMyCourt Admin account using any of the following methods:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the BookMyCourt Admin app
  2. Go to Profile (bottom navigation bar)
  3. Tap Account Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to support@bookmycourt.com with:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Your facility/business name

We will process your deletion request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once complete.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Your facility management settings and preferences
  • Staff member associations and role assignments
  • Push notification tokens and device registrations

What May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for legal, regulatory, or business continuity purposes:

  • Booking Records: Historical booking transactions may be retained for up to 3 years for legal and accounting compliance.
  • Settlement/Payment Records: Financial transaction records may be retained as required by applicable tax and financial regulations.
  • Player-Facing Data: Reviews and ratings left by players about your facility will be anonymized but may remain visible.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • If you are the sole admin of a facility, the facility listing will be deactivated upon account deletion.
  • Any active bookings at the time of deletion will need to be handled (completed or cancelled) before the account can be fully removed.
  • Uninstalling the app does not delete your account — you must follow the steps above.

For questions about account deletion, contact us at support@bookmycourt.com.
